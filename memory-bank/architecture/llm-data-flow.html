4. Product Onboarding — Data Sources

Subscribers add real products. System looks them up in public databases for verified ingredient data. Specific onboarding UI is separate.

Supplements — DSLD (NIH Dietary Supplement Label Database)

214,780 products (121,959 on-market). All major brands.

API: https://api.ods.od.nih.gov/dsld/v9/

Auth: None. CORS open. ~1,000 req/hour.

Data: Structured ingredients with amounts, categories, UNII codes, claims, form, manufacturer.

Search by: product name, brand, ingredient name.

Food — USDA FoodData Central

454,596 branded food products. Monthly updates.

API: https://api.nal.usda.gov/fdc/v1/

Auth: Free API key from data.gov. ~1,000 req/hour.

Data: Ingredients (text string), full nutrition, UPC barcodes, brand.

Search by: product name, UPC barcode.

Cosmetics — No good public API. MoCRA has 589K product listings but FDA hasn't made them publicly searchable. EWG Skin Deep has 130K products but no API. Onboarding falls back to manual entry: paste ingredient list, upload label photo, or describe product. LLM extracts structured data from manual input.

7. Key Design Decisions

  1. Products are the core unit. The email says "Your Marine Collagen Powder" not "This week in supplements." Product categories drive matching.
  2. Real product data from public databases. DSLD for supplements (214K products, structured ingredients), USDA FDC for food (454K products). Verified ingredient lists, not self-reported guesses.
  3. Cosmetics is the gap. No public product database. Onboarding uses manual entry (paste/upload/describe) with LLM extraction.
  4. Everything shows up, nothing is hidden. Paid emails show ALL items. Product-matched items get full analysis. Same-industry gets a brief. Everything else gets a one-liner + link.
  5. Free email is content marketing. Generic digest, same for everyone. Drives trial signups via product shot on homepage showing the personalized experience.
  6. Enrichment does the heavy lifting once. Deep tagging on affected ingredients, product types, claims, facility types happens at ingest. Email assembly just matches tags against product profiles.
  7. Three LLM providers for their strengths. Gemini for bulk enrichment (cheap, fast). Claude for writing (emails, search). OpenAI for embeddings.
